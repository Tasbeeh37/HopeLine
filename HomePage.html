HopeLine

Your Site To Improve Your Mental Health

OverView

HopeLine is aiming to create a safe space for teenagers who suffer from depression. Our goal is to break the stigma around mental health issues by educating and spreading awareness about the struggles that teenagers with depression face. HopeLine offers a free therapy-session booking service that makes reaching mental health professionals accessible to everyone. HopeLine is here to remind you that you are not alone, and that there is a community that cares for and supports you.
